# Calendar Clock Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 July 2026

Calendar Clock is a local-only browser extension. It reads Google Calendar events and timed Google Tasks that are already loaded in the page only to display them in the clock and local snapshot. The captured data stays on your computer and is not used outside the extension.

Calendar Clock does not send calendar data, task data, settings, or uploaded sounds to the developer, analytics providers, advertisers, or other external services. Diagnostics are copied to the clipboard only when you explicitly choose a copy action.

Use **Wipe app settings to default** or uninstall the extension to remove stored data. Privacy questions: forpphotos@gmail.com.
